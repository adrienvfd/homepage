My Story

I started my career as a Class A modeler in the automotive industry...
The role of the Class A surface modeler is to create 3D models of visible items of the car, taking into account the design requirements, including the designer'sketches, minimum number of Cvs, G2/G3 alignment, theoretical intersection construction, as well as the technical constraints such as gaps, clearance angles and minimum radius. The goal is to bring to 3D model up to ready for manufacturing quality.
